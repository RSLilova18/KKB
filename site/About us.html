ABOUT US



The KKB school system is still in beta version and every day we try to fix bugs and add new things.
You will not regret if you try to work with us.



We provide you a software completely free. Which you can use to manage, deploy and configure teams from your school.
Completely free and easy to download. We also have 24 hour support during the week if something happens by accident.
We will also have many online tutorials on our youtube channel where we will teach you how to use the site and software.
To keep up with improvements to the site and software can follow us on our social media. Such information will be uploaded there.



How to use our software:

Imagine we have a school as an example. The principal or teacher of the school uses
our software because you will have a project activity and you have to be assigned to teams.
So, they log in and download our software from the site. Once you use it and create the files.
They go back to the site on the "Teachers" page, where they upload and send the file and the
name and city of the school. Our team checks the file within 24 hours. And if everything is OK,
we upload it to the "Students" page, where all the lists of schools. To find the school easily, you can
use "The Find Bar" by clicking "F3" and then typing "School / City" to find it easily.If there are
any problems or questions or your file has not been uploaded within 24 hours, you can contact us
on the "Contact us" page, where you can send a message and we will reply to it
again within 24 hours.
